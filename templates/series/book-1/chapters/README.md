## Chapter Files

Chapters are stored as `[XX]-[title].md` where XX is the chapter number.

## Status Key

- **Not started** — No content
- **Outlined** — Beats/structure defined
- **Drafted** — First draft complete
- **Revised** — Revision pass complete
